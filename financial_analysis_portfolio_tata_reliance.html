Key Findings & Interpretation
Tata Motors showed strong recovery post-Covid, driven by JLR performance in international markets and EV adoption. High leverage (D/E 1.92×) poses solvency risk but is being reduced. DCF suggests 8.6% upside.
Reliance Industries demonstrated resilient, diversified revenue across O2C, telecom (Jio), and retail. Superior current ratio and low leverage indicate strong financial health. Near-fair valued at current price.
Trend Analysis revealed Reliance's consistent margin expansion while Tata Motors showed volatility in margins (FY20–FY22 losses) followed by sharp turnaround in FY23–24, presenting a classic turnaround story.
