Key Findings & Interpretation
Tata Motors showed strong recovery post-Covid, driven by JLR performance in international markets and growing EV adoption. High leverage (D/E 1.92×) poses solvency risk but is being actively reduced. DCF analysis suggests an 8.6% upside from current market price.
Reliance Industries demonstrated resilient, diversified revenue streams across O2C, Jio telecom, and retail. Superior current ratio and conservative leverage indicate robust financial health. The stock trades near its fair intrinsic value.
Trend Analysis revealed consistent margin expansion at Reliance while Tata Motors experienced sharp volatility (FY20–22 losses) followed by a strong turnaround — a classic recovery story supported by operational restructuring and JLR's return to profitability.
